# LunaFlow - Digital Wellness for Women

A dark feminine themed website for LunaFlow, a FemTech digital wellness application designed around women's biology.

## 🌸 About

LunaFlow bridges the gap between sophisticated data analytics and holistic health for women. Our algorithms analyze cortisol levels and cycle data to provide personalized insights for fertility tracking, hormonal balance, and fitness optimization.

## ✨ Features

- **Cycle Tracking** - Understand your monthly patterns
- **Hormonal Analysis** - Personalized insights based on your biology
- **Fitness Optimization** - Align your workouts with your cycle
- **Expert Resources** - Audio content from wellness experts

## 🎨 Design

This project features a dark feminine theme with:
- Rich dark color palette (deep plum/black tones)
- Rose gold and lavender accents
- Elegant typography (Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond)
- Glassmorphism effects
- Smooth animations
